## /rule

Disable Avahi Publishing

## /rationale

This
helps ensure that no record will be published by Avahi.

## /description

To
prevent Avahi from publishing its records, edit
`/etc/avahi/avahi-daemon.conf` and ensure the following line appears in
the `[publish]` section:

``` 
disable-publishing=yes
```
