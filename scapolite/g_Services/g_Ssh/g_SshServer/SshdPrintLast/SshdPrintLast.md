## /rule

Enable SSH Print Last Log

## /rationale

Providing
users feedback on when account accesses last occurred facilitates user
recognition and reporting of unauthorized account use.

## /description

When
enabled, SSH will display the date and time of the last successful
account logon. To enable LastLog in SSH, add or correct the following
line in the `/etc/ssh/sshd_config` file:

``` 
PrintLastLog yes
```
