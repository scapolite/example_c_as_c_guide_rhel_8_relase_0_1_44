## /rule

Ensure /var/log Located On Separate Partition

## /rationale

Placing
`/var/log` in its own partition enables better separation between log
files and other files in `/var/`.

## /description

System
logs are stored in the `/var/log` directory. Ensure that it has its own
partition or logical volume at installation time, or migrate it using
LVM.
