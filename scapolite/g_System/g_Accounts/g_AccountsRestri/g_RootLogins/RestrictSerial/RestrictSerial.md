## /rule

Restrict Serial Port Root Logins

## /rationale

Preventing
direct root login to serial port interfaces helps ensure accountability
for actions taken on the systems using the root account.

## /description

To
restrict root logins on serial ports, ensure lines of this form do not
appear in `/etc/securetty`:

``` 
ttyS0
ttyS1
```
