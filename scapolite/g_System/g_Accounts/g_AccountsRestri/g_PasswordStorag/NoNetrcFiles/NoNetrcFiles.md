## /rule

Verify No netrc Files Exist

## /rationale

Unencrypted
passwords for remote FTP servers may be stored in `.netrc` files. DoD
policy requires passwords be encrypted in storage and not used in access
scripts.

## /description

The
`.netrc` files contain login information used to auto-login into FTP
servers and reside in the user\'s home directory. These files may
contain unencrypted passwords to remote FTP servers making them
susceptible to access by unauthorized users and should not be used. Any
`.netrc` files should be removed.
